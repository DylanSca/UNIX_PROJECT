Welcome to the BLeague

List of teams

Thunder City Hawks

Known for their fast tempo and highlight plays, the Hawks formed about a decade ago when Thunder City merged two local clubs. They quickly became fan favorites thanks to their explosive offense.

Riverfront Raptors

A defensive-minded team built around toughness. They started as a community league squad near the river docks and grew into a respected regional contender.

Northshore Titans

The Titans were founded by a group of former college players who wanted a team that embodied strength and discipline. Their identity revolves around physical play and strong leadership.

Metro Blaze

Created during a citywide sports expansion, the Blaze became known for their fast-break offense and flashy guards. Their name reflects the “blazing” pace they’re famous for.

Iron Valley Wolves

Originating from a steel‑industry town, the Wolves built their reputation on grit and teamwork. Their “pack mentality” became a symbol of the city’s working-class pride.

Skyline Comets

A young, energetic franchise known for long-range shooting and speed. They were founded to represent the city’s growing tech district and its futuristic skyline.

Harbor City Knights

One of the oldest teams on this list, the Knights began as a semi-pro squad. Their identity is rooted in tradition, discipline, and strong defensive schemes.

Crimson Peak Bears

Based in a mountainous region, the Bears are known for their physical style and home-court advantage at high altitude. They started as a small-town team that grew into a regional powerhouse.

Solar Ridge Spartans

Founded by a local academy, the Spartans emphasize strategy and fundamentals. Their disciplined playstyle reflects their origins in a school known for athletics.

Emarald Bay Strom

A coastal team famous for unpredictable, high-energy play. They were formed after a major storm devastated the area, symbolizing resilience and rebirth.
